    When it comes to cars, maybe no one knows you have as many as Doug DeMuro through his writings. He is a revered and revered American auto columnist, writer, and columnist who wrote articles for The Truth About Cars and Jalopnik in the past . Currently, he writes for the Autotrader.com Oversteer blog . He has also diversified the channels through which he interacts with his audience by creating an eponymous YouTube channel.

    Doug had a lot going on in his life and we are going to tell you about them here starting with everything we know about his childhood life, education, career, finances, and family life. This article will also include facts we collected about him that you probably never knew.

    Doug DeMuro – Bio (Age)

    Douglas DeMuro was born and raised in Denver, Colorado; he was born on May 22, 1988. As a young school-aged child, Doug was enrolled at George Washington High School from where he graduated before continuing his college education. Douglas enrolled at Emory University in Atlanta, Georgia in 2006, where he studied economics. He graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in 2010.

    Prior to his graduation, Doug DeMuro began working with Ferrari of Denver as a Customer Service Technician from May to August 2008. Subsequently, he worked with Saturn of Decatur as a Sales Consultant from July to November 2009. Porsche Cars North America (PCNA) became his next work rate as an intern. He worked as a business development intern for dealerships from November 2009 to May 2010. After graduation, he started working in the company as a full-time writer. Then, from May 2010 to December 2011, he worked as an allocation specialist, and from January 2012 to 2013, DeMuro became manager of vehicle allocation at PCNA.

    His position at the time made him the exclusive authorized dealer for Porsche vehicles in the United States and on top of that he was the direct replacement for PCNA’s Director of Vehicle Distribution, made sales forecasts at retail, managed customer orders for the Porsche 918 Spyder and ensured prompt delivery of the vehicle to customers by working efficiently with field staff.

    Despite the so rapid increase in PCNA; becoming the company’s youngest ever manager, Doug Demuro decided to quit his job to devote more time to what he loved to do – writing.

    Douglas became a full-time writer in January 2013, and hasn’t backed down since. He started writing for Jalopnik before creating a series where he bought cars voted by his readers. He normally kept the cars with him for a year before getting another one. The series became what is known today as DougCar. His automotive writing career has seen him write for sites like AutoTrader.com and The Week. He also has two books to his name which are ‘Plays With Cars’ (2013) and ‘Bumper to Bumper’ (2016). The books have sold thousands of copies over the years.

    Family: female

    While Doug DeMuro was at Emory University in Atlanta, Georgia working as a resident counselor, he met a beautiful young woman who caught his eye. The two didn’t start dating but Doug made sure to keep his position as closest friend. This move worked out for him and he married her sometime in 2017 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The duo later moved to the San Diego metro area and have lived together ever since.

    In case you are wondering why we avoided mentioning his wife’s name, the above information is all there is to know about the writer’s wife. His name and other details of his identity remain a closely guarded secret by Doug. Due to Doug’s need for privacy, it is unknown whether or not they had children.
