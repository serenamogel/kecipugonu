    Robert Bruce was a member of the cast of “Comic Book Men,” a popular reality TV show.

    From 2012 through 2018, Comic Book Men was aired on AMC. The show featured Bruce in 34 episodes and was set at Kevin Smith’s comic book store, Jay and Silent Bob’s Secret Stash.

    Bruce worked on a total of 82 episodes as a producer and consultant. His popularity grew as a result of the AMC series, but he was already well-known as a collectables expert before he appeared on it.

    Robert Bruce Cause Of Death

    According to a report, Robert Bruce, a cast member of the popular reality TV show “Comic Book Men,” was discovered dead at a storage facility in New Jersey.

    After his family had not heard from him in several days, Bruce, 62, was discovered Friday at the City Center Plaza Professional Records Storage Facility in Red Bank.

    It’s unclear how long ago Bruce died, and the cause of death has yet to be determined.

    Many people have paid tribute to Bruce since the reality television star’s death was announced.
