    Demi Burnett (born February 21, 1995) is an American Interior Designer, Reality Television Personality and Instagram Star from Dallas, Texas. She is famous for appearing in the reality shows like “The Bachelor” and “Bachelor in Paradise“.

    Furthermore, the 24-years-old star initially appeared in the 23rd season of “The Bachelor” which aired on ABC Network. In fact, this season of the dating series featured “Colton Underwood” (charity founder, former football player, and TV personality) as the main lead.

    Moreover, the first episode of the show aired on the 7th of January 2019. Demi quit the show after the 6th week of the show. The ESL teacher and Speech Pathologist named “Cassie Randolph” came out as the winner of the dating show.

    Later, Burnett was chosen by the maker of the show to appear in the 6th season of the “Bachelor in Paradise“. Some of the other contestants are Dylan Barbour, Hannah Godwin, Caelynn Miller-Keyes, Adrianne Averbukh, Derek Peth, Wills Reid, Onyeka Ehie, Katie Morton, Tayshia Adams, Mike Johnson, etc.

    In fact, BIP Season 6 will premiere on the 5th of August 2019 on ABC Network. Last month on June 17, 2019, the first seven participants came out on the “Good Morning America”.

    Recently, a big twist on the upcoming reality TV show revealed on the internet. According to a source, Demi is in a relationship with her girlfriend “Kristian Haggerty”. She will not be dating any of Hannah Brown‘s ex-suitors or Becca Kufrin‘s ex-boyfriends during her time in the Bachelor in Paradise.

    In addition, she met her new love during her vacation trip to Mexico this summer. This is the first time that the reality series will showcase the romance between the same sex. However, Kristian is not a Bachelor Nation alum.

    Catherine Agro (The Bachelor Season 23 Contestant) and Haggerty remained roommates back in Florida, USA. Agro was the one who introduced Burnett and Kristian for the first time. In fact, they began their relationship back in February 2019.

    In April 2019, the new couple went to West Hollywood for an outing and also uploaded their pictures on Instagram. However, Demi never came out as bisexual publicly and declared her sexuality.

    Many sources also claim that she and Kristian went public with their affair during the show. By profession, Haggerty is an aspiring model, actress, and comedian. Currently, she works as a sales consultant for LightWork Software.
