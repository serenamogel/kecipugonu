    Which Jennifer Aniston Hairstyle Should You Go For?

    05 Aug 2024, 00:00

    From the defining "Rachel" haircut to the sensual styles on the red carpet, Jennifer Aniston's hair has been the talk of the town (and the world) for decades. Even I've tried to mimic many of her looks.

    With the launch of her haircare line LolaVie celebrating it's one year anniversary recently (the glossing deangler is my fave — you gotta try it) I decided to take a look back at the star's famous hairstyles throughout the years, and chose one look that will suit each of the zodiac signs.

    Discover which style of Aniston's you're destined to try in 2023, ahead.

    Aries

    Aries are constantly on the go, due to their fiery nature, which is why they will like wearing a hairstyle that has two braids — and a baseball hat of their choice. This sporty chic vibe will be the new look that Aries will want to rock everyday.

    Taurus

    If there is one thing Tauruses know, it's how to add glamour to their hair 24/7. This hairstyle from Aniston will bring major red carpet inspiration to the bull who's looking to level up their look. Not only does it sizzle and ooze seductiveness, but it's sophisticated and playful.

    Gemini

    Geminis tend to gravitate towards versatile haircuts that allow them to play around with different styles. This bob cut that Jennifer Aniston had will serve as inspiration for the airy sign to wear. The sleek style that radiates positivity can be worn half up, down, or in an updo.

    Cancer

    Cancers like to have their hair in an updo to ensure that they can show off their sensitive eyes to the world. Letting others see their emotions will be tough for Cancers, but once they give people the chance to know the real them — then their hearts will sing with glee.

    Leo

    Being that Leos are known to be fearless, they'll vie for these empowering curls that make a bold statement and demand attention. This effortless hairstyle has a sensual and beautiful vibe that will go with any outfit. Leo can dress up or down and be able to flaunt these curls.

    Virgo

    This hairstyle shows Jennifer's hair in its natural color and texture. Being that Virgos like to be au natural, they'll opt to mimic this stylish haircut and color that Jen wore back in the day. Even the outfit that is cute with her gorgeous chocolate brown locks and side part.

    Libra

    Being that the planetary ruler of Libra is Venus (the planet of beauty, who also governs hair), it is only sensible to assume that they will like to style their tresses like these gorgeous golden long locks. It'll be their everyday look — that goes with every outfit for every occasion.

    Scorpio

    Scorpios tend to like a more structured hairstyle and cut, but that doesn't mean they will forgo the radiance and bounce of a layered cut. This sign likes hairstyles that have a razor sharp edge, but still with a lot of soul, tenderness, and passion — much like Alex Levy on The Morning Show.

    Sagittarius

    Sags are known to the free-spirits and bohemians of the zodiac, which is why they'll be drawn towards this carefree hairstyle that radiates chicness and coolness with no fuss. The archer is confident enough to show off their true self, which is what these lovely tresses will help them do.

    Capricorn

    "The Rachel" hair look vibes most with the sign Capricorn because they opt for a structured look that is functional in both the office and after hours at the bar. This iconic hairstyle is chic with a dress, jeans, or a business suit, and can be worn all year long.

    Aquarius

    Aquarians don't let trends dictate their hairstyles. They'll only wear their hair in ways that allow them to be their unique selves — without conforming to what's trending on social media. However, the water-bearer is known to choose hairstyles that honor their hippy and non-conformist sentiments that speak to them.

    Pisces

    This cool "Rockstar" look is a wonderful vibe for the wondrous mystical fish Pisces to choose as their hairstyle. The feathery strands of hair perfectly shape her face and the soft, wispy bangs are totally rocker chic (very Stevie Nicks meets Nico) with a modern twist that is currently in style.
