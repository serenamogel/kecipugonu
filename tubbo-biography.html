    Who is Tubbo?

    British Tubbo is a Minecraft gaming YouTuber and Steamer. He is renowned for playing Minecraft on YouTube and streaming the same game on Twitch.

    Tubbo- Professional Career

    He started his YouTube journey with his main channel on 15th May 2018. However, he started uploading his video on the channel in September 2019. At the time, he also started streaming on Twitch.

    As the Minecraft content creator, he created the series including Hypixel Skyblock, Brockworth Season 2, SMPEarth, and Dream’s MP. Also, he has participated in the Minecraft Championships for YouTubers.

    On August 29, 2019, he created his second YouTube channel, TubboLIVE where he uploads similar Minecraft games.

    In the 9th MC Championship, he participated as the Team Lime Llamas. The time the overall team was placed in the 6th position. Last, in MC Championships 12, he participated as the Team, Red Rabbits.

    Currently, Tubbo has 976.5k followers on Twitch. Also, he has 424k subscribers with 2.9 million views.
