    Smash Ultimate Summit 6 Day 1 has Started, Here is the Schedule

    Smash Ultimate Summit 6 Day 1 has started and you can now watch it live on Twitch. Smash Ultimate Summit 6 runs from March 23-26, 2023, and is the final Smash Summit and Beyond The Summit event. [embedded content]

    13 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Smash Ultimate Summit 6 Day 1 has started and you can now watch it live on Twitch.

    Smash Ultimate Summit 6 runs from March 23-26, 2023, and is the final Smash Summit and Beyond The Summit event.

    Here is the schedule.

    Smash Ultimate Summit 6 Schedule

    Thursday, March 23rd: (PDT)

    • 10:00AM – Player Intros
    • 11:00 AM – Squad Strike
    • 3:30 PM – Lui$ vs. Skyjay Bo5 Last Chance Qualifier
    • 4:00 PM – Can Smashers Paint?
    • 4:30 PM – Damage Ratio Wheel
    • 6:30 PM – 4v4
    • 7:30 PM – Reaction Time Contest
    • 8:00 PM – Mafia

    Friday, March 24th:

    • 10:00 AM – Celebrity Match Bracket
    • 11:00 AM – Group A (4 matches)
    • 12:45 PM – Group B (4 matches)
    • 2:30 PM – Tweek Talks Live
    • 3:30 PM – Group C (4 matches)
    • 5:15 PM – Group D (4 matches)
    • 7:00 PM – Controller Remap Bracket
    • 8:30 PM – Mafia

    Saturday, March 25th:

    • 10:00 AM – Celebrity Match Bracket
    • 11:00AM – Group ABCD (8 matches + tiebreakers)
    • 5:00 PM – Gauntlet Stage (8 matches in 4 groups)

    Sunday, March 26th:

    • 10:00 AM – VIP Finals
    • 11:00 AM – Final Bracket (23 matches)

    We recommend checking it out.
