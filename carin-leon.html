    Career & Professional Life

    Leon’s solo popularity correlated with the success of his band. In 2017, their first significant success, “A Traves del Vaso,” topped a dozen Latin charts (it has been covered by several bands since). The next year, Leon released four solo albums: Desvelada con Banda y Mariachi, A Puro Pelo, Pa Las de Vidrio (all live), and Amanecida con Todo y con Todos. Musically, they were all similar to Grupo Arranke’s, but the gears were about to turn.

    In 2019, Leon launched the ground-breaking El Malo. The album received airplay throughout Mexico and the Southwestern United States due to its seamless melding of Mexican regional subgenres, from polkas and bandas to mariachis, rancheras, and corridos – often in the same song – as well as Leon’s hyper-traditional music filled with strings, horns, accordions, acoustic guitars, bass, and snare drums. After extensive solo and Grupo Arranke performing, he released the 34-track streaming collection Borrachera con Taka Takas in January 2020, accompanied by the live Endcerrados Perro Enfiestados in April.
