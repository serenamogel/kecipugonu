    Mink Brar is a German-Indian actress, model, and producer. She is known for her tremendous performances in Bollywood films and Indian television shows. She is also notable for appearing in the reality show named ‘Bigg Boss’ in 2012.

    Mink Brar has been in the field of acting since her teens when she was introduced by the veteran actor Dev Anand to showbiz. It was the time when she was just 13 years old.

    Career

    The acting enthusiast, Mink Brar never looked back since she started off her career. She has been giving heart-winning performances in a variety of movies and Indian TV shows for which she got huge acclaim.

    Mink Brar made her broad career by giving wonderful performances and getting positive responses from the viewers.

    Let’s recall her role in the Bollywood film named ‘Hum Apke Dil Mein Rehte Hain’ in which she was seen in a song alongside Anil Kapoor. In the film, she appeared as a supporting actress.

    Likewise, we can see her acting abilities in many Bollywood movies wherein she was seen as a supporting actress.

    The actress, Mink Brar has worked in movies, television shows, music videos, and television reality shows as well.
