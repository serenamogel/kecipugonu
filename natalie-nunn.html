    Natalie Nunn Biography

    Natalie Nunn is best known as Reality TV starlet, club promoter, model who has an estimated Net Worth of $325 Thousand. Reality personality who first appeared on the fourth season of Oxygen's Bad Girls Club. She returned in following seasons and appeared on other shows on the Oxygen network.  She was born on December 26,1984, Oakland, CA. Her own wedding was broadcast on reality TV.
