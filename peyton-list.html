    Peyton List is an American actress. She was born on 6th April 1998 in Los Angeles, California, USA. She is known for being a great actress and model. She had a deep interest in acting and modeling since her childhood, pursuing her interest, she started her career as a child model.

    She modeled for tween magazines and companies. She debuted through a film named ’27 Dressess’ released in 2008. She became famous for acting in the ‘Diary of a Wimpy Kid Film’ series. It was started in 2011 and ended in 2012.

    Career

    She started her career as a child model. She posed for magazines and companies. A list made her acting debut with the romantic film 27 Dresses (2008). She gained further prominence for playing Holly Hills in the Diary of a Wimpy Kid (2010, film series).

    She has millions of followers on her Instagram account. The list appeared as Emma Ross in the sitcom Jessie (2011-2015, TV series) alongside Debby Ryan and Cameron Boyce. She has also appeared as Emma Ross in the sitcom Bunk’d (2015-2021, TV series). She played Peggy in the comedy film Hubie Halloween (2020) alongside Adam Sandler.

    Besides acting, she is a recording artist. She released her first music single titled, “Liar Liar,” with its single release titled, “Dance Till We Die” from the feature film “Anthem of a Teenage Prophet” in theaters & On-Demand January 11th. List, who is the film’s lead, performed the track.

    On Aug 23, 2019, she released another single titled “Don’t Cry” an electro-pop anthem for healing heartbreak, with a debut music video ft Cannon. Dirty Canvas released her songs, home to American Authors and Young Rising Sons.

    With a dedicated fan base and immense talent, she mentors the next generation of young women who look to her for advice and inspiration. She believes in hard work, self-respect, and chasing your dreams, and those are messages she’s proud to share.
