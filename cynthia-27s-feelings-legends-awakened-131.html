    Cynthia's Feelings
    シロナの想い Shirona's Feelings

    Cynthia's Feeings (Japanese: シロナの想い Shirona's Feelings) is a Supporter card. It is part of the Legends Awakened expansion.

    Card text

    You can play only one Supporter card each turn. When you play this card, put it next to your Active Pokémon. When your turn ends, discard this card.
    Shuffle your hand into your deck. Then, draw 4 cards. If any of your Pokémon were Knocked Out during your opponent's last turn, draw 4 more cards.

    Release information

    A Cosmos Holofoil variant of this card was released in the Diamond & Pearl Collection in August 2008.

    In Japan, a promotional Cosmos Holofoil version was awarded as a prize for those who participated and won battles at 2008 Spring Battle Roads between March and April of that year. Only 2 of 5 scheduled tournaments were held due to a threat letter received by staff. This effected the distribution and population of both, this card, and the Miracle diamond.

    Battle Road Spring 2008

    Held:

    Hokkaidō conference: Sapporo Park Hotel, Sapporo on March 23, 2008.

    Kansai conference: Asia & Pacific Trade Center (ATC), Osaka on March 29-and 30, 2008.

    Canceled:

    Kantō conference: Sunshine City, Tokyo on April 12 and 13, 2008.

    Chūbu conference: Nagoya Congress Center, Nagoya on April 20, 2008.

    Kyūshū conference: Fukuoka Kokusai Center, Fukuoka on April 29, 2008.

    "Initially, the Kanto tournament (April 12, 13) of the "Pokemon Card Game Battle Road Spring ★ 2008", which was scheduled to be held at 5 venues nationwide for a total of 7 days from Sunday, March 23, 2008. Regarding (scheduled to be held on Sunday), I received a letter from an unknown sender stating that it would interfere with the holding of the tournament.

    In response to this situation, as a result of careful discussions with related facilities, we consider the safety of our customers to be our top priority, and we are very sorry, but we are sorry for the Kanto tournament and the Chubu tournament and Kyushu tournament scheduled after that. We have decided to cancel it. I am sorry that I cannot explain the details from the viewpoint that it will not interfere with the investigation, but I would appreciate it if you could forgive me.

    We sincerely apologize to the customers who have been looking forward to participating in this tournament."

    -https://pokeboon.com/jp/promo_event/battle-road-spring-2008/

    Trivia

    Cynthia can be seen holding a Luxury Ball.

    Origin

    Cynthia is the Champion of the Sinnoh Pokémon League.
