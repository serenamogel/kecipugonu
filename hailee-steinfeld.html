    Hailee Steinfeld is an American actress & a singer who was born on December 11, 1996. She became very famous face after playing role of Mattie Ross in True Grit (2010) as a result of that role, she was nominated for prestigious awards like the Academy Award and the BAFTA Award.

    Career

    After that, nobody was able to stop her from performing great acting in movies like Ender’s Game (2013) and Begin Again (2013).

    But, playing in Pitch Perfect (2015–2017) made her very famous among youth. Later she was casted for coming-of-age film i.e., The Edge of Seventeen (2016) which earned her a Golden Globe nomination.

    In 2018, Charlie role in Bumblebee film was truly appreciated that made her place in the entertainment industry forever.

    Hailee Steinfeld has also lent her voice to various animated characters:

    • Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018) as Gwen Stacy
    • Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse (2023)
    • Arcane (2021–present)

    Her portrayal as Emily Dickinson in the Apple TV+ comedy-drama series Dickinson (2019–2021) and took on the role of Kate Bishop in the Disney+ miniseries Hawkeye (2021) both of these marks her victory for her career.

    Personal Life

    Hailee Steinfeld was born in the Tarzana neighborhood of Los Angeles, California. She is the younger of two children with an older brother named Griffin. Her family tree have roots from fitness trainer and actor Jake Steinfeld as her paternal uncle. Former child actor Larry Domasin as her maternal great uncle.

    Hailee Steinfeld’s father is Jewish while her mother follows the Christian faith (which makes her very unique with both cultural values). She attended Ascension Lutheran School, Conejo Elementary, and Colina Middle School. Her education took a unique turn when she was homeschooled from 2008 until her high school graduation in June 2015.

    In her personal life, Hailee Steinfeld’s romantic journey has seen its ups and downs. She began dating Instagrammer Cameron Smoller in 2016, making their public debut as a couple at a Golden Globes party in early 2017.

    She started dating Irish singer Niall Horan in December 2017 after her break up with Cameron. Relationship with Niall also failed a year later. As of May 2023, Steinfeld has began her love life by dating NFL quarterback Josh Allen.
