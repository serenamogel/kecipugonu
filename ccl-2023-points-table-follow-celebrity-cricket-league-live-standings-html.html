    The sixth match of CCL 2023 had fans on the edge of their seats as the Bengal Tigers squared off against the formidable Telugu Warriors. With both teams showcasing exceptional skills in previous matches, the anticipation was palpable. The game lived up to expectations, delivering entertainment galore. Let’s delve into the points table and see how the teams are faring.

    Telugu Warriors Lead the Pack

    The Telugu Warriors have surged to the top of the table with an impressive four points and an enviable net run rate of 2.22Their emphatic victory over the Bengal Tigers, where they won by eight wickets in Jaipur on February 25, propelled them to this position. Meanwhile, the Bengal Tigers find themselves in sixth place, yet to secure a win in the championship. The face-off between these two teams was a closely contested battle, with Jishu Sengupta’s remarkable 83-run performance setting a challenging target of 115 for the Warriors.
